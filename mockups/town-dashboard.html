Component Legend

New components on the Town Dashboard:

• Pulse strength meter — what fraction of topic groups have declared (town coherence)
• Group grid — all civic topic groups with 3 visual states (declared/voting/idle)
• Declaration preview cards — truncated mandate + opinion mini-bar per group
• Full declaration list — complete text, approval %, public opinion buttons
• Escalation tracking — which declarations were sent to state level and their status
• Convergence signals — "3 other CT towns also declared" = amplification in action
• Summary stats — groups, declarations, active votes, citizens, opinions

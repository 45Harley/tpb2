Representative targeting — the endgame. Declarations + mandates + public opinions, aggregated by topic, scoped to a representative's constituents. "Beam to Desk" packages the data for delivery.

This is the coherent beam: scattered citizen voices → refined mandates → group declarations → aggregated pulse → focused on one representative's desk.

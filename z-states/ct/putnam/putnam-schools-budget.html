Staff Reductions & Changes

üîª Staff Reductions

To bring the budget from 10.88% to 4.66%, significant cuts were made:

Positions Reduced or Eliminated

  • School Psychologist ‚Äî Reduced to part-time (0.45 FTE)
  • Speech/Language Pathologist ‚Äî Reduced to part-time (0.45 FTE)
  • 2 Paraeducator positions ‚Äî Removed from budget
  • All permanent substitute teachers ‚Äî Eliminated (loss of health insurance, guaranteed daily work)

Coaching Positions Eliminated (6 total)

  • 3 Assistant Football coaches
  • 1 Head Girls' Soccer coach
  • 1 Assistant Boys' Basketball coach
  • 1 Track coach

District is exploring cooperative programs with other towns.

Budget Accounting Moves

  • Moved salaries from local budget to Medicaid account (not a long-term solution)
  • Moved 0.75 FTE custodian salaries to Food Services account
  • Using Non-Lapsing account for vehicle lease payments
  • Health insurance budgeted at 10% (lower than recommended)
  • Assumed more risk in special ed cost forecasting

Requested But Not Funded

‚ùå These positions were requested but NOT included in the budget:

Mental Health & Support Staff

  • Social workers for PES and PMS ‚Äî Additional mental health educators needed
  • Registered Behavior Technician ‚Äî To support specialized special ed programs
  • Student Services Administrator ‚Äî To help limit clinical placements

Academic Support

  • Paraeducators for math and reading intervention ‚Äî "Current staffing levels are significantly insufficient"
  • Elementary teacher ‚Äî To reduce Grade 4 class size (currently 23 students/class)
  • World Language Teacher for PMS ‚Äî No foreign language at middle school

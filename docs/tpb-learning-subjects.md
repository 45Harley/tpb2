## Putnam CT Representatives — Education Allies

### Federal (top targets for outreach)
- **Joe Courtney** (U.S. House, CT-2) — sits on **Education & Workforce Committee**, Higher Ed & Workforce Training subcommittee. Champions apprenticeship/job training. TPB fits his agenda directly. *Your district rep.*
- **Chris Murphy** (U.S. Senate) — sits on **HELP Committee** (Health, Education, Labor & Pensions). Education is a top-line campaign issue. College affordability, public ed reform.
- **Richard Blumenthal** (U.S. Senate) — education not his lead issue
- **Jahana Hayes** (U.S. House, CT-5, not your district) — **2016 National Teacher of the Year**, 15-year history teacher. Sits on Education & Workforce Committee. CT-wide education champion.

### State
- **Mae Flexer** (State Senate, 29th) — worked 3 jobs to put herself through QVCC and UConn. Personal stake in accessible education.
- **Chris Stewart** (State House, 51st) — new, elected 2024. Limited record.

### Priority outreach: Courtney + Murphy
Both sit on the committees that matter. Courtney is local (CT-2). Murphy has the Senate platform.

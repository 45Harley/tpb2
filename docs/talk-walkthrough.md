### How to use it

1. **Tap the mic** or just start typing in the text box
2. **Pick a category** â€” "Idea" is selected by default. Use "Question" if it's a question, "Todo" if it's an action item, etc.
3. **Choose a group** (optional) â€” logged-in users see a dropdown to assign the idea to a group, or leave it as "Personal (no group)"


### Tips
- **Voice works great** for capturing thoughts on the go â€” just tap the mic, speak naturally, and it fills in the text
- Thoughts are **private by default** â€” nobody sees them until you mark them shareable
- **Group selector** â€” choose which group your idea belongs to. Ideas saved to a group are automatically shareable. Ideas stay personal by default.
- **Ctrl+Enter** (or Cmd+Enter on Mac) submits without clicking the button
- The "Reaction" category is grayed out here â€” it activates when you're replying to someone else's thought
- **Log in to keep your thoughts.** Anonymous thoughts are tied to your browser tab â€” close it and you can't find them again. Logged-in thoughts are tied to your account and persist forever.

| Control | What it does |

### Inviting members

Facilitators can invite people by email directly from the group detail page:

1. Scroll to the **"Invite Members"** section (facilitator-only)
2. Enter email addresses â€” one per line or comma-separated
3. Click **"Send Invites"**
4. Results appear inline with color-coded status for each email:
   - **Green** (Invited) â€” email sent with accept/decline buttons
   - **Red** (Invalid email / No account found) â€” can't invite
   - **Orange** (Already a member / Already invited / Email not verified) â€” skipped

Each invitee receives an email with two buttons: **"Yes, I'll Join"** (green) and **"No Thanks"** (gray). Clicking either link works without logging in â€” the token authenticates the response.

The **Invitations** list (visible to members and facilitators, not observers) shows all invites with status badges:
- **Pending** (orange) â€” waiting for response
- **Accepted** (green) â€” joined the group
- **Declined** (red) â€” chose not to join
- **Expired** (gray) â€” 7-day window passed with no response

Facilitators can re-invite someone who declined (creates a new invitation).
